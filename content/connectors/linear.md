# Connect Linear

About 2 minutes. Lets Claude pull status from your Linear workspace.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **Linear** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in and authorize.

<Checkpoint id="connector.linear.added">Linear is connected.</Checkpoint>

## Try it now

```text
Give me a one-page weekly status from Linear: top 5 shipped, top 5 at-risk, and any tickets older than 30 days.
```

```text
Look at the [TEAM] team's cycle. Which tickets seem mis-scoped (too vague, no acceptance criteria)? Suggest tightening for each.
```
