# Connect HubSpot

About 3 minutes. Lets Claude read CRM, marketing, and pipeline data.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **HubSpot** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in via OAuth. Pick the HubSpot portal if you have multiple.

<Checkpoint id="connector.hubspot.added">HubSpot is connected.</Checkpoint>

## Try it now

```text
Give me a one-page weekly GTM snapshot from HubSpot: new MQLs, SQLs, demos booked, demos held, closed-won, closed-lost. Highlight the metric most different from last week.
```

```text
Take the top 20 deals by amount in HubSpot. For each, give me the next-best-action in one sentence.
```
