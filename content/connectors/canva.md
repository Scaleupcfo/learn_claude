# Connect Canva

About 2 minutes. Lets Claude open, edit, and generate designs in your Canva account.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **Canva** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in with the Canva account that holds your brand kit.

<Checkpoint id="connector.canva.added">Canva is connected.</Checkpoint>

## What Claude can do

- 🖼️ Generate new designs (one-pagers, social posts, simple decks).
- 🎨 Apply your **Brand Kit** automatically so output looks on-brand.
- 🪄 Take an existing Canva design and propose copy and layout edits.

## Try it now

```text
Create a one-page sales sheet in Canva for [PRODUCT]. Audience: [BUYER]. Three sections: what it is, why it matters, how to start. Use my Brand Kit.
```

```text
Open the Canva design at [URL]. Rewrite the headline to be more specific, and propose a tighter layout.
```
