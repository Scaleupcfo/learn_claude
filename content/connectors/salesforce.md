# Connect Salesforce

About 5 minutes. Lets Claude read pipeline, accounts, and opportunities. Most useful for CEOs/CROs/CFOs reviewing GTM.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **Salesforce** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in via OAuth. On managed orgs, your Salesforce admin may need to approve the Anthropic connected app — show them this page.

<Checkpoint id="connector.salesforce.added">Salesforce is connected.</Checkpoint>

## Try it now

```text
Give me a one-page pipeline review: total open ARR by stage, top 10 deals by value, deals that slipped from last quarter, and any with no activity in 14+ days.
```

```text
Build me a 2-paragraph account brief on [ACCOUNT NAME]: who they are, history with us, current opportunities, and the right opening question for next week's call.
```
