# Connect Slack

About 2 minutes. Lets Claude summarize channels and draft messages.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **Slack** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in to your Slack workspace. If your workspace requires admin approval, request it — most orgs allow Claude.

<Checkpoint id="connector.slack.added">Slack is connected.</Checkpoint>

## What Claude can do

- 🔎 Search messages and channels you can see.
- ✍️ Draft DMs and channel messages — sent only with your approval.
- 🧾 Summarize a noisy channel into a 5-bullet brief.

## Try it now

```text
Summarize what happened in #leadership in the last 24 hours. Three buckets: decisions made, decisions pending, FYI.
```

```text
Draft a Slack message to #all-hands announcing [NEWS]. Tone: warm but professional. Under 120 words. Include one specific call-to-action.
```
