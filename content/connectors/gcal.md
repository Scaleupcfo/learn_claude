# Connect Google Calendar

About 2 minutes. Lets Claude see your schedule and propose meetings.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **Google Calendar** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in with the relevant Google account; approve calendar access.

<Checkpoint id="connector.gcal.added">Google Calendar is connected.</Checkpoint>

## What Claude can and can't see

- ✅ Read events, busy/free times, meeting details.
- ✅ Draft new events; with your approval, create them.
- ❌ Won't change or delete existing events without explicit confirmation.

## Try it now

```text
What does my week look like? Highlight the three meetings that need the most prep, and tell me what I should review before each.
```

```text
Find a 30-min slot in the next 5 business days that works for me and [PERSON@EMAIL]. Propose 3 options.
```
