# Connect Google Drive

About 2 minutes. Lets Claude open, read, and reference files in your Drive without you copy-pasting.

## Add the connector

1. Open [claude.ai/settings/connectors](https://claude.ai/settings/connectors).
2. Find **Google Drive** and click **Connect**.
3. Sign in with the Google account, approve file access.

<Checkpoint id="connector.gdrive.added">Google Drive is connected.</Checkpoint>

## What Claude can and can't see

- ✅ Search and read files you share with it (or grant blanket access to).
- ✅ Create new files in folders you authorize.
- ❌ Will not delete or move files without your explicit approval.

## Try it now

```text
Open the most recent doc in my "Board" folder and give me a one-page executive summary plus a list of any open questions.
```

```text
Find every spreadsheet I touched in the last 14 days and tell me what KPIs they track.
```
