## ② How to iterate

- Test the JD against 3 candidate profiles in your head. If a great candidate would skip it, the JD is too generic.
- Ask: *"Rewrite the JD so it's clear within 30 seconds whether someone is qualified."*
- For interview questions, ask: *"Generate two more — one for grit, one for self-awareness."*

## ③ How to launch the kit

**Claude Cowork:** assemble, copy into your ATS (Greenhouse, Lever, etc.).

## ④ When things go wrong

- **JD reads like every other JD** → *"Cut every cliche. No 'rockstar', 'fast-paced', or 'wear many hats'."*
- **Questions feel basic** → *"Make each question harder and more specific to our stage."*
