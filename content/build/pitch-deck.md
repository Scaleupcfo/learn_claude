## ② How to iterate

- After draft 1: *"For each slide, give me the 'so what' in one line. If any slide doesn't have one, cut or rewrite."*
- Test the story by reading just the headers. They should tell the whole pitch on their own.
- Ask: *"What's the strongest objection a partner would raise? Add a backup slide that answers it."*

## ③ How to create the pitch deck

**Claude Cowork** + **Google Slides** is the fastest path.
**Claude Code** if you want everything in version control: generate a markdown outline first, iterate, then push to Slides/PowerPoint.

## ④ When things go wrong

- **Bland** → *"Show me the most distinctive sentence on each slide. If a slide doesn't have one, find it."*
- **Numbers feel weak** → *"For the traction slide, find the one number that's most defensible and lead with it. Hide the others."*
- **Generic team slide** → *"For each person, write a 5-word reason they're the right founder."*
