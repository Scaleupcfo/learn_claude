# Welcome

You're about to spend roughly three hours getting comfortable with two products: **<Term>Claude Code</Term>** (Claude with hands — on your computer, in your tools) and **<Term>Claude Cowork</Term>** (Claude in the browser, alongside your team).

By the end you will have:

- Installed Claude Code on your machine
- Wired the connectors you actually use (email, calendar, drive, design, etc.)
- Built at least one real artifact across four common CXO categories:
  communications, presentations, numbers, and operations
- A reusable mental model and playbook so you can keep going on your own

The whole experience is paced for ~3 hours. You can leave at any time and pick up where you left off.

> **A note before you start.** You will feel awkward in the first 15 minutes. That's normal. Once you ship the first artifact, the awkwardness becomes confidence. Don't optimize prematurely — just keep going.
